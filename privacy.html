Privacy Policy

See You Soon Events does not collect, store, or share personal data. All events, photos, and countdown information remain on your device.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, third-party trackers, or user accounts.

Приложение не использует аналитику, рекламу, сторонние трекеры или аккаунты пользователей.
